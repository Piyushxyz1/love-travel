Corbett Tour Packages

Corbett National Park is a pristine paradise, which is found in the foothills of stunning Himalayas. Being one of India’s most well-known wildlife sites, it bears the name of a famous British hunter, who later became environmentalist, Jim Corbett. The park spans over 520 square kilometers and is an enduring testament to the beauty and variety of nature. Corbett has something remarkable to offer everyone, from nature lovers to adventure seekers and from family travellers to corporate partners, depending on their individual needs.

Our Corbett tour packages are developed just for enthusiastic travellers who are looking for a unique experience amidst pure natural beauty and amazing wildlife sightings. Every visitor will be able to experience the charm of this magnificent location thanks to the flexibility and affordability of our Corbett Wildlife tour packages. Whether looking for a weekend getaway or a long vacation, our tour packages are suitable for all types of visitors. Our packages also provide the opportunity to see the splendour of other significant locations nearby Corbett and tend to deliver the finest of all.

One can schedule a Corbett tour at any time of the year, even though November to June is the park’s busiest time for wildlife adventures. The journey will be comfortable and hassle-free for the travellers by choosing the right package from the extensive range of packages to make the most of the time and money. Our Corbett tour packages, spanning from 2 to 16 days, cater to a diverse array of tourists — from passionate wildlife enthusiasts to family vacationers, and from corporate groups to other collective travelers. Each package is tailored to meet specific needs, ensuring everyone gets the best experience. Moreover, our tours offer the opportunity to explore the captivating beauty of sites around Corbett, and some even combine the best of North India with the unique charm of Corbett National Park.
